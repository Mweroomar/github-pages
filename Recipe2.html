Pilau Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g meat, cubed
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons pilau masala
  • 4 cups water or broth
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil

Instructions:

  1. Brown meat in oil until well cooked
  2. Add onions and garlic, saut√© until golden
  3. Add pilau masala and stir well
  4. Add rice and stir to coat with spices
  5. Pour in water or broth and add salt
  6. Bring to boil, then reduce heat
  7. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes
  8. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving

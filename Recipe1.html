Chapati Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour and salt in a large bowl
  2. Add water gradually and knead into soft dough
  3. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes
  4. Divide dough into small balls
  5. Roll each ball into flat circles
  6. Heat pan over medium heat
  7. Cook each chapati until brown spots appear
  8. Apply oil and serve hot
